About

LifeRPG is a game that fuses your personal agenda with an interactive role playing game, where your life, your daily tasks and your social network become a RPG based game. The successful completation of your tasks will get you Experience (XP), so you can level up and get new stuff within the app. You decide what are your awards are. e.g: You just got home from grocery shopping, and that was one of your tasks? Reward yourself with an ice cream bar! Experience increases your progress bar, and molds your stats graph, based on the categories of your tasks. You can manage your exercise schedule, your shopping trips and more!
